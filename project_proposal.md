# PROJECT PROPOSAL DOCUMENT

## Manikanta Trading Company -- E-Commerce Website

------------------------------------------------------------------------

## 1. Introduction

The rapid growth of digital technology and online shopping has
transformed traditional businesses into modern, scalable platforms.
*Manikanta Trading Company*, a trading business dealing in groceries and
wholesale commodities, aims to expand its operations by developing a
fully functional e-commerce website.

This platform will enable customers and retailers to browse, order, and
purchase products online, thereby increasing reach, efficiency, and
revenue.

------------------------------------------------------------------------

## 2. Objectives

-   To develop a user-friendly e-commerce website\
-   To provide online access to grocery and wholesale products\
-   To support both retail (B2C) and wholesale (B2B) customers\
-   To simplify order management and inventory tracking\
-   To increase business visibility and sales

------------------------------------------------------------------------

## 3. Scope of the Project

### User Features

-   User registration and login\
-   Product browsing\
-   Add to cart and checkout\
-   Multiple payment options\
-   Order tracking

### Dealer Features

-   Bulk pricing\
-   Large quantity ordering

### Admin Features

-   Manage products, orders, users\
-   Inventory tracking

------------------------------------------------------------------------

## 4. Technology Stack

-   Frontend: Vite + React.js\
-   Backend: Node.js\
-   Database/Auth/Storage: Supabase\
-   Payment: Stripe

------------------------------------------------------------------------

## 5. Conclusion

This project will modernize business operations and improve customer
reach.
